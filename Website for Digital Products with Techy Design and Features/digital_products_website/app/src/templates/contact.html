Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex websites with custom functionality may take 6-12 weeks or more.

How much does SEO cost?

Our SEO packages start at $799 per month for basic services. The cost depends on your business goals, competition level, and the current state of your website. We offer customized solutions to fit your specific needs and budget.

Do you offer ongoing website maintenance?

Yes, we offer website maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support.

How do I know if Facebook Ads are right for my business?

Facebook Ads can be effective for most businesses, but the strategy and approach may vary. During our initial consultation, we'll assess your business goals, target audience, and competition to determine if Facebook Ads are a good fit and how to optimize your campaigns for success.

What information do you need to get started?

To get started, we typically need information about your business, your goals, target audience, competitors, and any existing brand assets. We'll provide a detailed questionnaire and schedule a kickoff meeting to gather all the necessary information.
